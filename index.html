Tutorial

Hello, this is a small tutorial before you dive into the game.

  • The game is a simple 'double or nothing' game with an incremental twist. Each time you press the 2x button, you either lose money or double your betting amount.
  • The win percentage is given in the top right corner. You can increase the win percentage by buying more.
  • Once your win percentage is higher than 80%, you can buy +0.5 multiplier. But beware, it will reset the progress and you will start with a lower win percentage.
  • Your objective is simple: increase the multiplier as much as you can.
  • The game officially ends when you unlock the 4.5x multiplier.
